About Us

Mas Oyama Kyokushinkaikan is managed by Sosai Mas Oyama's family to protect his legacy and teachings. Dedicated to preserving the authenticity of Kyokushin karate, we safeguard his rights, materials, and vision. This site offers an official hub for supporting his work, connecting practitioners, and celebrating Sosai's enduring impact.
